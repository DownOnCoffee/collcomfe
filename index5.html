Two Factor Authentication and Strong Password


  • Gmail and all Social Media platforms including WhatsApp are giving a security feature of securing the login account using Two- Factor or Multi factor Authentication.
  • Two factor authentication means once you enter user id and password to login your account, then it ask for One Type Password (OTP) which is sent on your registered phone number to confirm the authenticity of the user
  • Enabling this feature will help account to protect from hacking.
  • Always use combination of number, alphabets and special symbols to create Strong Password.
