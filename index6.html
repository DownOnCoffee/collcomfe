OLX Scam


  • The fraudsters either send a QR code or a request to accept payment on the pretext of buying the item listed by the victim.
  • In some cases, they show unnecessary urgency and are willing to pay even without checking the item, claiming that they don’t to miss out on the deal.
  • Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal’s daughter was recently a victim of an online scam where she tried to sell an old sofa set on OLX but was dupped of Rs 34,000 by an online scammer

How to deal with KBC (Kaun Banega Crorepati) scammers?


  • KBC doesn’t send you such texts on WhatsApp, so if you receive a text, complain to the police.
  • If you receive a call, disconnect it immediately.
  • If you observe the message, logo and poster carefully, you’ll realize that it is fake
  • Scammer can send any request of seeking for help for cancer or any disease treatment or lottery winning message or any such kind of emergency help over email or WhatsApp, please do not entertain the message and be careful.
  • If you receive such texts, report to www.cybercrime.gov.in
